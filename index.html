About Us

One of our marvellous ramen.

Noodle

Our main focus is on ramen noodles, delicious firm wheat based noodles served in a tasty broth. At Noodle you will find traditional Japanese flavours with a local twist. Savouriness, taste, flavour, that is our philosophy, our way of living. We try to source the best Japanese and local products to produce the tastiest ramen possible. Taste is what it is all about at Noodle, cooking with beautiful products, Tender Loving Cooking, obviously ;-)
