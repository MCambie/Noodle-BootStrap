About Us

One of our marvellous ramen.

Noodle

Our main focus is on ramen noodles, delicious firm wheat based noodles served in a tasty broth. Please do not expect a menu with 100 items at our restaurants, we prefer to focus on one thing and doing it well. At Noodle you will find traditional Japanese flavours with a local twist. Savouriness, taste, flavour, that is our philosophy, our religion, our way of living. We try to source the best Japanese and local products to produce the most nutrient, healthiest, but above all the tastiest ramen possible. Noodle is slow (cooked) food, served fast. So yes, taste is what it is all about at Noodle, cooking with beautiful products, served with integrity and loads of TLC, Tender Loving Cooking, obviously ;-)
